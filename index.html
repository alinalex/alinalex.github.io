About me

I’m a self-taught web developer from Europe doing front-end development @ejump. My projects involve toying around with HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, Worpress, PHP, Photoshop and Skectch.

My life story started when my parents bought me my first lego. Since then, I have loved to make stuff (happen) and I kept seeking a craft that would allow me to do just that.

Last year I decided to give another shot to my seeking adventure and I quit my job to learn how to program by my own - the full story is on medium.

Fast forward 10 months after that and I had an AHA moment while reading a book in the subway. Basically, I have loved solving problems for my entire life but it took me 24 years to realise that this is exactly what designers do and that's my craft I want to master.

But, how did I get to quit my job and start learning programming? Well, my professional trajectory is full of diverse experiences and you can check my resume for that.

For the next years I want to broaden my design skills because I want to learn and put in practice as much as I can about UX and to be a product designer.

In my spare time, I love to read (Nasim Taleb is one of my favourites), to stay in shape, to watch tennis, listening to Kendrick Lamar songs, spending quality time with my better half and of course, to better my craft at designing stunning UX.
