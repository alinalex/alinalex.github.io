About me

I’m a self-taught front-end developer/web developer/programmer from Europe doing front-end development @ejump. My projects involve html5 css3 jQuery worpress php and photoshop.

My life story starts when my parents bought me my first lego. Since then I have loved to make stuff (happen) and I kept seeking for a craft that would allow me to do that.

I decided to give another shot to my seeking adventure and last year I quit my job to learn how to program by my own (you can read the full story on medium). Fast forward 10 months after that and I had the AHA moment while reading a book while riding the subway. Basically, I have loved solving problems for my entire life but it took me 24 years to realise that this is exactly what designers do.

But, how did I get to quit my job and start learning programming? Well, my professional trajectory is quite unique (I have even been to US before I got into college at a FEE summer seminar). My background is in finance, I did some volunteering by organising a project for students, I have worked in the financial industry, I have also been into consultancy and I started a business with a friend of mine in the second year of college, which failed. For the long version you can check out my resume.

For the next years I want to broaden my design skills because I want to do UX and be a product designer.

I love to read - taleb is one of my favourites - , playing tennis (mostly watching), listening to kendrick lamar songs, spending quality time with my better half and of course, to better my craft at designing stunning UI’s.
